Delete Your Smart Health Monitor Account

Last Updated: 15 July 2026

Smart Health Monitor gives every user the ability to permanently delete their account and associated personal data. This page explains how to request account deletion, what information is deleted, and what information may be retained.

Delete Your Account Using the App

You can permanently delete your account directly from within the Smart Health Monitor application.

  1. Open Smart Health Monitor.
  2. Sign in to your account.
  3. Go to Settings.
  4. Select Account.
  5. Tap Delete Account.
  6. Confirm the deletion request.

Once confirmed, your deletion request is processed immediately.

What Data Is Deleted

Deleting your account permanently removes your personal information from Smart Health Monitor, including:

  • Your account profile
  • Your authentication account (Firebase Authentication)
  • Health readings and monitoring history
  • Medicine schedules and reminders
  • Doctor and caretaker links
  • Clinical notes and prescriptions associated with your account where applicable
  • Push notification tokens associated with your account

Data That May Be Retained

Certain information may be retained only where required:

  • Temporary server logs required for security, fraud prevention, or system reliability.
  • Information required to comply with applicable legal obligations.
  • Backup copies that are automatically removed according to our backup retention schedule.

We do not retain your personal health information longer than necessary after account deletion unless required by law.

Deletion Timeline

Account deletion requests are initiated immediately after confirmation. Most user data is removed promptly, although complete removal from backups or disaster recovery systems may take up to 30 days.
